Chatobby Obsidian Connector - Third-Party Notices
Last updated: 16 July 2026

The production connector assets are built from Chatobby-owned source and use
Obsidian and Node.js APIs supplied by the host application. The Obsidian API is
externalized from main.js and is not redistributed in the connector assets.

The reviewable source uses build and test dependencies recorded exactly in
package-lock.json. Those development dependencies are not shipped in the
three-file connector release. Their license metadata and source are available
from their respective package distributions.
